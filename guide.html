How to Play Simon Game

  • Starting the Game

    Press the "Start" button to begin the game. Your score will appear on the screen.

  • Watch the Colored Buttons

    Pay attention to which color lights up and makes a sound. Remember it!

  • Your Turn to Repeat the Sequence

    Click the same color button that just lit up. If youâ€™re right, another color will be added to the sequence. Now, remember the first color and the new one.

  • Keep Following the Pattern

    Repeat the sequence of colors correctly to keep earning points. Your score is shown at the top of the screen.

  • Game Over

    If you click the wrong button, the game ends with a "Game Over" message. Press "Restart Game" to try again.

  • Sound Controls

    Click "Mute" to turn off the sound. Use the volume slider to adjust the sound level.

  • High Score

    Keep track of your highest score on the screen. Try to beat it each time you play!

  • How to Play Button

    If you need a reminder, click the "How to Play" button to see these instructions again.
